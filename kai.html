ATTHEHEART

The inspiration for KAI came from the same place most of greatest achievements have—from a conversation with my 4 year old son, Kai. We were sitting in the office of his speech therapist filling out a stack of papers, in an attempt to get another stack of paper for our upcoming daycare tours. Kai has Autism and Type 1 Diabetes, both of which he handles with such a gentle spirit and sometimes force him to exercise his patience—a patience that has never extended to his curiosity.


For families like mine life is so riddled by paperwork it really does feel like a second job sometimes. KAI is a love letter to families and center owners/directors alike because it's designed with you both in mind. I'm not a silicon valley tech startup, I'm a mom and entrepreneur who built an app for moms and entrepreneurs alike.

Kayla, Image & Co Founder

KEY FEATURES

Ouch Log

Streamlined incident reporting, with real time parent notifications and photo documentation.

Digital Enrollment

Piles of paperwork are a thing of the past—with field detectors, e-signature capabilities, and easy document uploads.

Intact E-checkin

Parents can ensure that their kids are in good health and have been dropped off/picked up by authorized persons with two clicks.

Audit Dashboard

Center personnel can quickly prepare for audits and recall records easily, with customizable toggles and parameters you'll have a downloaded file ready for print and review in 15 seconds or less.

Messaging + Photos

Whether it's a center wide blast, quick question for a teacher, or just a photo of a young art prodigy's latest creation, you can do it all.

2-Factor Medical

Named SASF (Special Alerts for Special Friends), this system triggers a pulse and sound alert to confirm medication dosage or session start.

Classroom Mode

Lock designated devices into classroom mode; no sensitive information is available without your pin to switch into admin mode.

Bank-Level Security

In compliance with state laws children's records are deleted within 3-7 years, while we do store records we can ensure that all data is stored securely and encrypted.
