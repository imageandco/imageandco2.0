At the Heart of it all

The inspiration for KAI came from the same place most of greatest achievements have—from a conversation with my 4 year old son, Kai. Kai has Autism and Type 1 Diabetes, both of which he handles with such a gentle spirit. For families like mine life is so riddled by paperwork it really does feel like a second job sometimes.


My job is being able to identify pain points, so I did—childcare is one of the few things destined to affect most families. KAI is a love letter to families and center owners/directors alike because it's designed with you both in mind. I'm a mom and entrepreneur who built an app for moms and entrepreneurs alike. Launching February 26.


With the best regards, Kayla, Image and Company Founder and CEO.
