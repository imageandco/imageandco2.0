## **Featured Product: KAI (Keeping All Intact)**
**KAI** is our flagship Childcare Companion SaaS, designed specifically for childcare center owners and directors. 
* **The Problem:** Most childcare software is built by VCs, not providers, and often ignores medical inclusion.
* **The Solution:** A "Brightwheel alternative" built by a mom-developer. KAI features specialized medical alerts (SASF), digital enrollment, and bank-level security.
* **Mission:** Empowering Memphis childcare centers and national providers with a black-owned, medical-inclusive software solution.
